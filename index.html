My ultimate research goal is to build more effective Human-AI communication layers. This involves enhancing users' ability to describe and clarify their real-world context when interacting with AI systems, maximizing co-performance in Human-AI collaboration, and enabling AI systems to learn from the true rewards of real-world human tasks. In my view, AI should serve to form a deep partnership with humans, enhancing their capabilities rather than supplanting them.

As a researcher with an interdisciplinary background, my MSc major is Mechanical Engineering from SJTU's School of Design (advised by Prof. Danni Chang). I have collaborated with leading researchers such as Prof. Xin Yi (Tsinghua University) on LLM privacy and Dr. Qiao Jin (Carnegie Mellon University) on AIED.
