Here are some core problems I‚Äôm thinking about:

  • Human-AI Collaborative & Adaptive Systems: How to optimize the delegation of tasks to AI while preserving space for human-intensive decision-making?
  • Human Autonomy and Control: How to create AI systems that can seamlessly integrate with human workflows while maintaining human autonomy and control?
  • Human-AI Scaffold to Improve Foundation Reasoning Ability: How to use human-AI collaboration data to train agents that can think while doing?

In my view, AI should serve to form a deep partnership with humans, enhancing their capabilities rather than supplanting them.
